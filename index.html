About me

Loukman Lamany is a Business Development Analyst at National Community Reinvestment Coalition (NCRC), a national non-profit organization with the mission to create opportunities for people to build wealth. At NCRC, Loukman works for the Small Business Teaming Center where he advises small businesses on winning contracts with local, state, and federal governments. Prior to NCRC, Loukman interned as a Program Strategist at Athgo International, where he was in the program coordinator of Athgo’s Youth Program during the 2013 Transform Africa Summit in Kigali, Republic of Rwanda. A youth and education spokesperson, Loukman appeared on Huffington Post Live and spoke at the Capitol Hill on behalf of City University of New York -Accelerated Studies in Associate Program and the American Youth Policy Forum (AYPF). Loukman is a graduate of CUNY – Baruch College.
